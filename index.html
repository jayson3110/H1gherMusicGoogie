WOOGIE / 우기

그의 시그니쳐 사운드 ‘WOOGIE on and on’이 들리면 일단 믿고 듣는 라인업의 곡들과 힙합부터 발라드, R&B까지 장르를 가리지 않고 오래오래 들을 수 있는 음악들을 만들고 싶다는 H1GHR MUSIC의 대표 프로듀서 ‘WOOGIE


2018 멜론 뮤직어워드 핫트렌드상을 수상한 ‘주지마-로꼬, 화사(2018)’를 프로듀싱 하며 대중들에게 자신의 시그니쳐 사운드와 이름을 알린 WOOGIE는 이외에도 ‘영화 한 편 찍자-페노메코(2019)’, ‘낙서 (Feat. 백예린)-펀치넬로(2019)’, ‘KITKAT (Prod. WOOGIE)-Woodie Gochild, 김하온, 식케이, pH-1(2018)’, ‘향수 (Prod. by WOOGIE)-우원재(2017)’, ‘RING RING (Feat. 개코)-식케이(2016)’, ‘남아있어 (Feat. Crush)-로꼬(2016)’, ‘생각해 (Feat. 박재범)-레디’ 등을 프로듀싱 했으며, 아티스트의 매력을 최대치로 끌어 올려주는 프로듀서로 계속해서 러브콜을 받고 있다.


또한 본인의 감성을 녹여낸 EP [REWIND MY TAPE part.1(2018)], [REWIND MY TAPE part.2(2018)]와 ‘Better (Feat. Golden)(2020)’, ‘가을속에서-화사(2019)’, ‘웰컴 투 서울 (Feat. Ugly Duck, pH-1, 박재범)(2018)’, ‘PLAY ME (Feat. 식케이, PENOMECO)(2018)’, ‘ROLLING STONES (Feat. 카더가든)(2018)’ 등의 싱글 작업물도 꾸준히 발표하고 있다.


음악, 그 자체를 오래 하고 싶다는 WOOGIE는 본인의 감각과 결정이 명확하다는 것을 믿는다는 확고한 신념을 가진 뮤지션 이다


His signature sound ‘WOOGIE on and on’ is a stamp of quality from H1GHR MUSIC’s main producer WOOGIE. He wishes to make timeless music regardless of genre, ranging from hip-hop to ballad and R&B.


Sik-K made himself known to the public by performing on the AOMG team on Show Me The Money 4. After appearing on the show, he continued his music career with his crew YELOWS MOB and became a founding member of Jay Park’s H1GHR MUSIC


After receiving the Melon Music Awards Hot Trend Award in 2018 for producing ‘Don’t-Loco, Hwasa(2018)’, WOOGIE made his signature sound and name known to the public. Additionally he produced ‘Movie-PENOMECO(2019)’, ‘Doodle (Feat. Yerin Baek)-punchnello(2019)’, ‘KITKAT (Prod. WOOGIE)-Woodie Gochild, HAON, Sik-K, pH-1(2018)’, ‘Nostalgia (Prod. by WOOGIE)-Woo Won Jae(2017)’, ‘RING RING (Feat.Gaeko)-Sik-K(2016)’, ‘Still (Feat. Crush)-Loco(2016)’ and ‘Think (Feat. Jay Park)-Reddy’, and is recognized as a producer that brings the best out of artists


In addition, Sik-K has shown great performances with H1GHR MUSIC artists on songs such as ‘GANG Official Remix(2020)’ and ‘iffy (Prod. GroovyRoom)(2017)’. He has engraved his mark on the public and shown his desire to not only create hip-hop and rap music, but represent the entire youth with hits such as ‘RING RING (Feat. Gaeko)(2016)’, ‘Rendezvous(2016)’ and ‘party (SHUT DOWN)(Feat. Crush)(2017)’


In addition, WOOGIE released his own EP [REWIND MY TAPE part.1(2018)], [REWIND MY TAPE part.2(2018)] and singles ‘Better (Feat. Golden)(2020)’, ‘In The Fall-Hwa Sa(2019)’, ‘Welcome To Seoul (Feat. Ugly Duck, pH-1, Jay Park)(2018)’, ‘PLAY ME (Feat. Sik-K, PENOMECO)(2018)’, ‘ROLLING STONES (Feat. Car, the garden)(2018)


WOOGIE, who wants to make music for a long time, is a musician with a firm belief that his senses and decisions are clear.
